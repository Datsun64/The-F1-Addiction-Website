Sunday 8th May 2022

Max Vertsappen snatches victory away from Pole Sitter Charles Leclerc after starting in p3 and lauching the car fast enough to overtake Carlos Sainz in the first corner! The podium is then completed by the Ferraris of Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz respectively. Sergio Perez managed to complete his race in p4, despite having a sudden power loss midway through the race while almost overtaking Sainz. A good race from the duo Mercedes George Russell and Lewis Hamilton both finishing p5 and p6, after struggling in the previous races for double point finishes. Valtteri Bottas is the sole Alfa Romeo Driver to bring the car into the points, but he could have been ahead of both Mercedes cars if he didn't send his car too deep into Turn 17. Farnatsic recovery from Esteban ocon, who manages to pring his car p8 after starting last in p20! A criminally underrated performance that truly went under the radar of a lot of people. Fantastic race from both alex Albon and Lance Stroll who managed to bring their Williams and Aston martin respectively in the last position of achievable points.
